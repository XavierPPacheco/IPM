Bubbles Express

Our app consists of an uber-inpired laundry cleaning service. You put your laundry in a basket and put it in the designated drop of point where a pickup truck will pick it up, put in the dry cleaners and return it promptly. Its main users are threefold: students that either don’t have time or don’t know how to do their laundry, city-residents that don’t have the proper conditions to do so and people that just want to not have the work. Not mutually exclusive of course!
